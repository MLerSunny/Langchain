CALIFORNIA AUTO INSURANCE POLICY
Policy Number: CA-AUTO-12345
Effective Date: January 1, 2025

SECTION 1: COVERAGES

A. LIABILITY COVERAGE
   We will pay damages for bodily injury or property damage for which any insured becomes legally responsible because of an auto accident. 
   Limits: $100,000 each person, $300,000 each accident for bodily injury; $50,000 for property damage.

B. MEDICAL PAYMENTS COVERAGE
   We will pay reasonable expenses incurred for necessary medical and funeral services because of bodily injury caused by accident.
   Limit: $5,000 per person.

C. UNINSURED MOTORISTS COVERAGE
   We will pay compensatory damages which an insured is legally entitled to recover from the owner or operator of an uninsured motor vehicle.
   Limits: $100,000 each person, $300,000 each accident for bodily injury.

D. COMPREHENSIVE COVERAGE
   We will pay for direct and accidental loss to your covered auto not caused by collision or rollover.
   Deductible: $500 per occurrence.

E. COLLISION COVERAGE
   We will pay for direct and accidental loss to your covered auto caused by collision or rollover.
   Deductible: $1,000 per occurrence.

SECTION 2: EXCLUSIONS

We do not provide coverage for:
1. Intentional damage caused by you or a family member
2. Damage that occurs while your covered auto is being used as a public or livery conveyance
3. Damage to property owned by or being transported by an insured
4. Damage to a vehicle used in any racing event
5. Loss due to war, nuclear hazard, or radioactive contamination

SECTION 3: DEFINITIONS

A. "Covered auto" means:
   1. Any vehicle shown in the Declarations
   2. Any of the following types you acquire during the policy period:
      a. A private passenger auto
      b. A pickup or van with a Gross Vehicle Weight of less than 10,000 pounds

B. "Collision" means the upset of your covered auto or its impact with another vehicle or object.

C. "Bodily injury" means bodily harm, sickness or disease, including death that results.

SECTION 4: PREMIUM CALCULATION

Your premium is calculated based on:
1. Driver age and experience
2. Vehicle type and usage
3. Location of primary garaging
4. Prior claims history
5. Coverage selections and deductibles

SECTION 5: POLICY PERIOD

This policy applies only to accidents and losses that occur during the policy period shown in the Declarations.

ENDORSEMENT CA-001: CALIFORNIA GOOD DRIVER DISCOUNT
If you qualify as a "Good Driver" under California law, you are entitled to a 20% discount on your premium.

ENDORSEMENT CA-002: RENTAL CAR REIMBURSEMENT
We will pay up to $30 per day, to a maximum of $900 for transportation expenses incurred by you in the event of a covered loss to your auto.
